Excursion and Conference dinner

For the excursion we will visit the historic area of Dazaifu, located southwest of Fukuoka city. We will take you on a guided tour to the following spots:

  • Dazaifu Tenmangu: https://www.dazaifutenmangu.or.jp/en/
  • Kyushu National Museum: https://www.kyuhaku.jp/en/

After the excursion, we will come back to the center of the Fukuoka city, and go directly to the venue for the conference banquet by chartered bus. Because the venue is in the city center, you can come to the conference banquet on your own in case you do not join the excursion. The name of the banquet venue is “Daimyo Conference”. It is close to the Akasaka subway station, and is 8-10min walk away from the conference venue ACROS Fukuoka.

  • Daimyo Conference: https://daimyo-conference.jp/en/access/

Timetable on June 26th

  • 9:00-12:00 Sessions in the morning
  • 12:00-12:50 Lunch at the conference venue

    • We will provide lunch boxes (for you and also your accompanying persons who are assigned extra tickets).

  • 13:00 Leaving from the conference venue

    • Excursion at the Dazaifu area

  • 18:15 Arriving at the Daimyo Conference (banquet venue)
  • 18:30 Opening Conference banquet
  • 20:30 Closing Conference banquet
