Venue

The venue for CPM 2024 is the ACROS Fukuoka, Japan.

The full address is 1-1-1 Tenjin, Chuo-ku, Fukuoka, Japan.

Access to the conference room

The closest subway station to the venue is Tenjin. There is a direct pathway from the Tenjin subway station to the 2nd underground floor (B2F) of the ACROS Fukuoka. Take Exit 16 to get to the venue. Move up to the 1st floor (1F, the ground floor) by stairs, lift, or escalator. From the 1st floor, take a lift to the 7th floor.
